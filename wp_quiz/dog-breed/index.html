What Dog Breed Are You?
German Shepherd

Intelligent and hardworking. Known to be extremely versatile and courageous.
Golden Retriever

Family-friendly with a fantastic demeanor. Love to run and play, they’re a great fit for families with young children.
Bulldog

Bulldogs are fun and loveable. They are very calm. Great companion, prefer to stay home and relax most of the time.
Beagle

Peaceful and cheerful. A hunting breed with great curiosity, these hounds enjoy exploring and keeping their noses active.
Yorkshire Terrier

Extremely devoted to their owners, brave and energetic breed.
Poodle

Regular appearances in dog shows, their personalities are just as charming as their looks. Poodles are smart, easy to train, eager to please, and lots of fun to be around.
Rottweiler

Large and powerful. Very intelligent, easily trained, and willing to work. Rottweilers have even bigger hearts and make for devoted companions.
Boxer

Have endless energy and love to play. They are extremely loyal and great with kids.
