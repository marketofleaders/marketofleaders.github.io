Unexpected Kitchenware Hacks You Need To Know

7. Cutting Boards in Poor Condition

Whether they are made of wood or plastic, they have a shelf life. Yours may exhibit proud hundreds of cuts and spots, but your kitchen, food and your health will thank you for renewing them.

6. Food That You Will Never Cook

They tend to be at the back of the closet. And it is not that you have forgotten (that too), it is that for one reason or another you never cook them. Take a deep breath, immerse yourself in the pantry and rescue those packages and boats that either brought you on a trip -like the pasta that comes from the Italian getaway or bought you on impulse.

5. Pastry Accessories That You Have not Used in 3 Years

Yes, okay, you’d love it, you carry it inside, but you never take it out. You keep molds, kitchenware of the most varied, pastry sleeves, muffin papers and you never bake anything.
Arm yourself with courage and give away what you are not going to use.
Surely you have someone around who can give them the best use.

4. Unpaired Food

A loose plate of a dish that disappeared and you never take out, a forgotten Jarret, a chipped soup tureen, check your kitchenware and get rid of the pieces that are broken or unpaired.

3. The Twenty Thousand Glass Jars You Keep

Every time you drop one in your hands you clean it and keep it. To fill them with homemade jam, fried tomato, leftover broth, freeze lentils. But some have traces of glue on the labels, caps somewhat rusted on the inside or the chipped edge. Stay alone with those who are in better condition and the sizes that you use the most.

2. Expired Spices

It seems you like the little bottles of spices! With those colors, in mini size, but do you use tarragon in your dishes? It is not about getting rid of what you use, but there are condiments that you would never put in your recipes. And what’s more, some accumulate years of life. Check dates and leave a gap. Learn How to Build Your Spice Rack

How many trays do you really use? One, two, three, four as much? Take a minute to think about it, how many you have and how many times you use them. And now, be consistent and unmade the oldest ones or those in the worst state.

1. Cleaning Products You do Not Need

Because we tend to store almost finished product bottles, samples, too many, and scourers … You need four products (a little more) to keep the house like the paten. For this, it is essential that the clothes and wipes are in good condition.

Remember to get rid of plates, cups, and glasses that are peeled. Remember that life is only one. So, use the beautiful kitchenware you have every day. It is not worth living to store for special occasions and see damaged objects every day.
