Kitchenware You Really Need

16. Funnel

A funnel is to avoid browning when you have to transfer liquids from one place to another.

Funnels are the perfect addition to your cooking tools, use for refilling spices, transferring liquids, pouring hot oils and adding powders to mixtures and shakes. There are Easy to Clean – Resistant to stains and odors; wash with warm water and soap.

15. Textiles

Other indispensable and basic kitchen accessories are the aprons, the oven gloves, the clothes or towels, and the tablecloths and the individual pieces. It is an excuse for the incorporation of customized designs into your kitchen.

14. The Sharp Cutters

The knives always cause a little bit of printing. More when you saw many horror movies in adolescence. Anyway, the best thing is to put them in a safe place. They are within reach, but fixed, quiet, and away from the smallest of the house.

A proposal that meets these requirements is that a magnetized bar can be the perfect solution to have several knives dry and close, without presenting a risk of accidents. AMAZON PRICE: $17.99

13. Spices

Surely you were wondering where the spice farmers are? If you like to cook, spices are essential in your kitchen. There are as many designers such as accessories designers and interior decorators this planet can offer. Glass spices are a good option, not only from the practical point of view but also as a source of color in the kitchen. AMAZON PRICE: $19.97

12. Cool Things

The cutter tears our soul according to its shape. It can be from the heart or from any other design that you love; maybe a bit dramatic. The important thing is to have something original, be it a toaster in a funny way, a novelty napkin, a pin or magnet for the refrigerator that moves you and take you out of the routine for a second; to open the game, the fan, to add salt and pepper to your everyday kitchen adventure, to mention but a few.

11. Utensils

The image above gives you an accurate picture of how cooking utensils should be aligned to achieve harmony in the kitchen environment. It is an example of a distinctly modern kitchen, from a minimalist classic with marble gray and dark wood in a delicate contrast that shines as much as possible with utensils and accessories made of glass, aluminum or white ceramic.

For the rest, a touch of color would not be bad, perhaps in ceramics, perhaps through textiles. The materials of the accessories and kitchen utensils go hand in hand with the styles and preferences that each house and each environment needs.

10. Silicone Spatula

By adopting the minimalist style, maybe you want to avoid having a wooden spoon, a fork and also a spatula, choose a silicone spatula instead and forget about the rest. AMAZON PRICE: $19.98

9. Ladle

Having a ladle in your kitchen is necessary. Anyone who does not have it has burned himself by serving soup with a cup. Perfect for portioning and serving soups, stews and chili. AMAZON PRICE: $6.99

8. A Can Opener

Maybe you want to open a can of tomato paste, or maybe you want something spicy and you prefer coconut milk to make a curry. The easy-open is not so easy even when it is not standard throughout the world, this is where a can opener is inevitable. AMAZON PRICE: $14.95
