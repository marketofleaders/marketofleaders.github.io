Recipe 1: Classic Irish Coffee

Here is yet another Irish inspired coffee that I know you are going to want to make over and over again. It is somewhat strong so be braced for that.

Yield: 1 Serving
Preparation Time: 3 Minutes

List of Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup of Water, Warm
  • 2 Tablespoons of Whiskey, Irish Flavored
  • Some Dessert Topping, Your Favorite Kind
  • 1 ½ Tablespoons of Coffee, Instant Variety
  • Dash of Brown Sugar, Light, Packed and for Taste

Instructions:

  1. Using your favorite coffee mug, add your water and instant coffee into it and stir thoroughly to combine.
  2. Add in your remaining ingredients except for your dessert topping and stir thoroughly until evenly mixed.
  3. Serve with your dessert topping and enjoy right away.

Recipe 2: Southern Style Coffee

There is nothing quite like the taste of coffee from the southern states. This coffee is bold in flavor and will definitely give you the energy you need in the morning.

Yield: 2 Servings
Preparation Time: 3 Minutes

List of Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups of Milk, Whole
  • Sugar, As Much as Your Like
  • 1 Cup of Louisiana Style Coffee, Made with Chicory Preferable

Instructions:

  1. First pour your milk into a small sized saucepan. Heat over high heat and bring the milk to a boil. Once your milk is boiling, remove it from heat.
  2. Using your favorite coffee mug, combine your boiling milk and coffee together and stir to incorporate.
  3. Sweeten your coffee with as much or as little sugar as you like.

Recipe 3: Pumpkin Pie Spiced Latte

If you are a huge fan of pumpkin pie spiced…anything, then this is a recipe I know you are going to want to make over and over again.

Yield: 2 Servings
Preparation Time: 5 Minutes

List of Ingredients:

  • 4 Tablespoons of Pumpkin, Canned
  • 3 Tablespoons of Syrup, Vanilla
  • ¼ teaspoons of Spice, Pumpkin Pie Variety
  • 1 tablespoon of Syrup, Pumpkin Spice Variety
  • 2 Cups of Milk, Whole
  • 3 to 4 Shots of Espresso

Instructions:

  1. The first thing that you will want to do is use a small sized saucepan and stir together your milk and pumpkin puree over medium heat.
  2. Heat your mixture thoroughly until it begins to steam and foam.
  3. Once it is foaming, pour your pumpkin mixed milk into a tall drinking glass and pour your espresso over the top.
  4. Garnish your drink with your pumpkin pie spice and whipped cream if you desire.

Recipe 4: Mexican Style Coffee

If there is one thing that most native Spanish countries are known for, it is how they brew their coffee. With this delicious coffee recipe in your kit, you now can make one of the most popular types of coffee around.

Yield: 2 Servings
Preparation Time: 5 Minutes

List of Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups of Coffee, Your Favorite Kind and Brewed
  • ½ Cup of Milk, Whole
  • 4 Tablespoons of Honey, Raw
  • 1/8 teaspoons of Cinnamon, Fresh and Ground
  • Dash of Nutmeg, For Taste
  • Dash of Vanilla, For Taste

Instructions:

  1. Place all of your ingredients together in a small sized saucepan.
  2. Heat over medium heat just until your mixture is hot. Make sure that it is not boiling.
  3. Pour into a coffee mug and enjoy immediately.

Recipe 5: Easy Frappuccino

If you are a fan of classic café drinks, then you are going to love this recipe for yourself. It makes for a tasty drink that you can make for your guests during your next friendly gathering.

Yield: 1 Serving
Preparation Time: 5 Minutes

Ingredients for Your Frappuccino:

  • 1 teaspoon of Sugar, White
  • ¼ teaspoons of Pectin, Dry
  • ¼ Cup of Milk, Cold and 2% Variety
  • ¾ Cup of Coffee, Espresso Variety

Ingredients for Your Flavoring:

  • 1 teaspoon of Cocoa, Unsweetened Variety
  • 1 tablespoon of Coffee Flavoring, Vanilla or Almond Preferable
  • 1 teaspoon of Baking Flavoring, Liquid Variety and Cinnamon Preferable

Instructions:

  1. Add at least ¾ of a cup of your hot coffee and your desired sugar into a blender. Blend on the highest setting for the next 30 minutes or until your sugar is fully dissolved.
  2. While your blender continues to run, pour in your remaining ingredients.
  3. Continue to blend for at least an additional minute before serving. Enjoy!

Recipe 6: Around The World Inspired Coffee

If you have ever wanted to taste all of the different flavors of coffee that exist around the world, then this is the perfect coffee recipe for you. I guarantee once you serve this coffee up in your household, all of your guests are going to love it.

Yield: 10 to 12 Servings
Preparation Time: 5 Minutes

List of Ingredients:

  • 6 teaspoons of Coffee, Instant Variety
  • 4 Tablespoons of Cocoa, Unsweetened Variety
  • 1 teaspoon of Cinnamon, Ground
  • 5 Tablespoons of Sugar, White
  • Some Whipped Cream, For Topping

Instructions:

  1. The first thing that you will want to do is mix all of your ingredients together except for your whipped cream. Mix until thoroughly combined.
  2. Brew your coffee with your mixture according to your preference and serve with a dollop of whipped cream.

Recipe 8: Classic Mocha Frappe Iced Coffee

If you are a huge fan of Iced coffee and are looking for one of the tastiest coffee recipes on the planet, then this is the perfect iced coffee for you.

Yield: 2 Servings
Preparation Time: 5 Minutes

List of Ingredients:

  • ½ Cup of Espresso, Freshly Brewed and Chilled Completely
  • 6 Tablespoons of Syrup, Chocolate Flavored
  • 1 tablespoon of Sugar, White
  • ½ Cup of Milk, Whole
  • 1 Cup of Ice Cream, Vanilla Flavored
  • ¼ Cup of Heavy Cream, Whipped Lightly
  • Some Chocolate Curls
  • Dash of Cinnamon, Ground

Instructions:

  1. First place you’re your first 4 ingredients into a blender and blend on the highest setting until thoroughly combined.
  2. Then add in your ice cream and continue to blend until smooth in consistency.
  3. Pour your drink into chilled glasses and serve with a topping of a dash of cinnamon and fresh chocolate curls. Enjoy right away.

Recipe 9: Easy Coffee Frappe

Here is another great tasting coffee recipe that I just know you are going to fall in love with. This makes for the perfect pick-me-up that you can make right before you head out to work in the morning.

Yield: 4 Servings
Preparation Time: 3 Minutes

List of Ingredients:

  • 18 to 22 Ice Cubes, Finely Crushed
  • 8 Ounces of Coffee, Double Strength Variety and Completely Chilled
  • 2 Tablespoons of Sugar, White
  • 2 Tablespoons of Flavoring, Syrup Variety and Your Favorite Kind
  • Dash of Whipped Cream, For Garnish

Instructions:

  1. The first thing that you will want to do is place all of your ingredients except for your whipped cream into a blender.
  2. Blend on the highest setting until your frappe is smooth in consistency.
  3. Once smooth pour into a large sized drinking mug and garnish with your whipped cream.

Recipe 10: Cinnamon Spiced Coffee

Here is yet another great tasting cinnamon spiced coffee recipe that I know you are going to want to make over and over again.

Yield: 4 Servings
Preparation Time: 5 Minutes

List of Ingredients:

  • 4 Cups of Coffee, Strong and Freshly Brewed
  • 1 Stick of Cinnamon, Broken into Small Pieces
  • ½ Cup of Cream, Heavy Variety
  • Some Coffee Syrup, Your Favorite Kind

Instructions:

  1. Using a small sized saucepan pour your freshly brewed coffee over your broken up stick of cinnamon and allow to stand for at least 1 hour.
  2. After this time remove your cinnamon sticks.
  3. Stir in your cream and allow to chill completely.
  4. Serve your drink in chilled glasses and serve with as much coffee syrup as you like and enjoy immediately.
